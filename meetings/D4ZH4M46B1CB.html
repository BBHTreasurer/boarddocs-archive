Special Meeting

May 22, 2024

Brecksville-Broadview Heights Education Center6:00 p.m. The BBHCSD Board of Education will be holding a Special Meeting on Wednesday, May 22, 2024 at 6:00 p.m. at 6638 Mill Road, Brecksville, OH 44141. This is a Special Meeting of the Board of Education in public for the purpose of reviewing and approving the five-year forecast update, personnel recommendations, a revision to BBH Administrative Guidelines, K-5 ELA textbook adoption, and to consider the tentative agreement with the Brecksville-Broadview Heights Organization of Support Staff. This not to be considered a public community meeting. There will be no public participation at this meeting.
