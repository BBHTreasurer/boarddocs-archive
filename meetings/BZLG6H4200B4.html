Special Meeting

April 6, 2021

Brecksville-Broadview Heights Education Center 6 p.m. This is a Special Meeting of the Brecksville-Broadview Heights Board of Education in public for the purpose of considering the Superintendent's employment contract(s), and to consider a resolution regarding payment in lieu of transportation and HVAC upgrades/building improvement(s) in compliance with the Elementary and Secondary School Emergency Relief Fund to address the impact of COVID-19.This meeting is not to be considered a public community meeting. There will not be an opportunity for public participation.~6p.m. ~ Special Meeting

2.A Contract Addendum Action

WHEREAS, the Brecksville-Broadview Heights City School District Board of Education (“Board”) and Joelle M. Magyar (“Superintendent”) entered into an Employment Contract, effective August 15, 2019 through July 31, 2022; and WHEREAS, the Board and Superintendent desire to modify the Employment Contract.NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Board President is authorized and directed to execute an addendum to the contract of employment with Ms. Magyar on behalf of the Brecksville-Broadview Heights City School District Board of Education, upon the terms and conditions as agreed; and BE IT FINALLY RESOLVED, that it is hereby found and determined that all formal actions of this Board concerning and relating to the adoption of this resolution were adopted in an open meeting of this Board in compliance with all legal requirements, including Section 121.22 of the Ohio Revised Code.

2.B Roll Call Procedural
2.C Employment Contract Action

WHEREAS, ORC Section 3319.01, provides for the appointment of a superintendent for a city school district; and WHEREAS, Joelle M. Magyar (“Magyar”) has agreed to continue in the position of Superintendent for the Board of Education of the Brecksville-Broadview Heights City School District (“Board”), in accordance with the terms of a contract of employment. effective August 1, 2022. NOW, THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, that the Board employ Joelle M. Magyar as Superintendent for the term beginning August 1, 2022 and extending through July 31, 2025; andBE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Board President is authorized and directed to execute a contract of employment with Ms. Magyar on behalf of the Brecksville-Broadview Heights City School District Board of Education, upon such terms and conditions as agreed and as may be required to bring about the legal effect of the terms of this Resolution; and BE IT FINALLY RESOLVED, that it is hereby found and determined that all formal actions of this Board concerning and relating to the adoption of this resolution were adopted in an open meeting of this Board in compliance with all legal requirements, including Section 121.22 of the Ohio Revised Code.
