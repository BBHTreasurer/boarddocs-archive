Meeting Summary:

 

The Brecksville-Broadview Heights City School District Board of Education convened on December 18, 2024, at 6:00 PM.  Following the Pledge of Allegiance and roll call, the Board addressed a comprehensive agenda.  Discussions encompassed updates on career technical education (CTE) programs, legislative developments, and financial reports, including ongoing audits. 

 

Committee reports were presented, and the Superintendent and Treasurer provided their announcements.  The Board also allocated time for community communications and reviewed new policies, with a focus on administrative guidelines and the appointment of Mark Dosen as president pro tem.

 

Funding for the upcoming fiscal year's CTE partnership was approved, along with revisions to the high school course catalog.  The Board authorized bids for necessary middle school roof replacements and sanctioned trips for the 8th-grade class to Washington, D.C., and the high school marching band to Orlando. 

 

Personnel matters were addressed, including both certified and classified positions, along with supplemental recommendations.  The Board concluded its business by approving financial reports, authorizing tax advances, adjusting the budget, accepting donations, and approving minutes from the preceding meeting. 

 

The meeting was adjourned at approximately 7:30 PM, with well wishes for a happy and safe holiday season extended to all.
