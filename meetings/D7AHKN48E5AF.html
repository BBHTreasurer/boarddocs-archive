Meeting Summary:

 

This is a summary from a meeting of the Brecksville Broadview Heights Education Center Board of Education on August 21, 2024.

 

-In the meeting, the board members unanimously approved all items on the agenda. The President, Mark Dosen, welcomed everyone back for a new year and mentioned that the audit was in progress. The board discussed various committee reports, including the Schools Foundation report, which highlighted the addition of a new member, Keith Richardson, and their ongoing efforts to update their documentation and bylaws. A new initiative by the Sustainability Committee was also mentioned, with details to be presented at a future meeting.

-The board also discussed the PSO report, which included details about the membership drive and the benefits of joining. The Superintendent, Jeff Harrison, introduced a composting initiative in partnership with Rust Belt Riders, a for-profit business that provides composting services and soil sales. The initiative aims to  reduce waste and educate students about environmentally sound practices.

-The board also discussed the transportation policy, school meal prices, and the approval of personnel recommendations. The Treasurer, Craig Yaniglos, presented the financial report and discussed the annual reporting of commercial paper. The board also accepted donations and approved the minutes from the previous meeting.

 

-The meeting concluded with the board entering an executive session.
