2. Election of a President for 2020 (accept nominations, no second needed, then ballot)

Mr. Dosen nominated Mrs. Mack.  There were no other nominations.

Motion by Mr. Dosen to close nominations and cast a unanimous ballot for Mrs. Mack as Board

President for 2020.

ROLL CALL:  Mr. Dosen, yes; Mrs. Kramer, yes; Mrs. Mack, ABSTAIN; Mr. Pedersen, yes; Mrs. Wigman, no                   

Mr. Dosen declared Mrs. Mack President for 2020 and declared Resolution 2020-01 adopted.

 

Mr. Pedersen will now turn the meeting over to the new President.

 

3. Election of a Vice President for 2020 (accept nominations, no second needed, then ballot)

Mr. Pedersen nominated Mr. Dosen. Mrs. Wigman nominated Mr. Pedersen

Motion to close nominations and cast a ballot for Mr. Dosen as Board Vice

President for 2020.

ROLL CALL:  Mr. Dosen, yes; Mrs. Kramer, yes; Mrs. Mack, yes; Mr. Pedersen, yes; Mr. Wigman, no                   

The President declared Mr. Dosen Vice President for 2020 and declared Resolution 2020-02 adopted.

 

4. Order of Voting on Motions and Resolutions in 2020

Motion by Mr. Dosen, seconded by Mrs. Kramer to adopt Resolution 2020-03 as follows:

The member order of voting shall be in alphabetical order with the exception of the Board President who will vote last.

Robert's Rules of order shall prevail in determining all board meeting procedural questions.

ROLL CALL:  Mr. Dosen, yes; Mrs. Kramer, yes; Mrs. Mack, yes; Mr. Pedersen, yes; Mrs. Wigman, yes                   

The President declared Resolution 2020-03 adopted.

 

5. Treasurer/CFO's and Superintendent's Business

Motion by Mr. Dosen, seconded by Mr. Pedersen to adopt Resolution 2020-04 as follows:

Resolution to authorize the Treasurer/CFO of the Brecksville-Broadview Heights City School District to request the

County/Auditor to advance taxes from the porceeds of the tax levies pursuant to Section 321.340 of the Ohio Revised

Code to meet current expenses as needed during the period of January 1, 2020 through December 31, 2020;

AND FURTHER RESOLVE under authority of Section 3313.20 O.R.C. that the Board dispense with approval of ordinary bills

for purchases made under authority of the annual appropriations resolution and permit payment as seems advantageous;

AND FURTHER RESOLVE to waive the reading of the minutes of the Board as authorized by Amended House Bill #424;

AND FURTHER RESOLVE to authorize the Treasurer/CFO to invest funds that are not needed to meet current expenditures - Section 135.09 O.R.C.;

AND FURTHER RESOLVE to authorize the Treasurer/CFO to make appropriate modifications to the budgets as necessary during the year. A listing of

modifications will be submitted to the Board monthly;

AND FURTHER RESOLVE to appropriate a $20,000 Service Fund account in 2020;

AND FURTHER RESOLVE to authorize the Treasurer/CFO to approve blanket purchase orders up to a maximum of $10,000;

AND FURTHER RESOLVE to grant authority to the Superintendent to excuse teachers and other school employees for attendance at,

and provide all approved expenses for, professional and/or in-service meetings in 2020;

AND FURTHER RESOLVE to grant authority to the Superintendent to approve expenditures for those fringe benefits and other forms of

non-salary remuneration not prohibited by statute, per formal opinion OAG81-052 clarifying the issue of non-salary remuneration for public employers;

AND FURTHER RESOLVE to grant the Superintendent or her designee authority to file applications for all projects considered desirable for the

Brecksville-Broadview Heights City School District;

AND FURTHER RESOLVE to authorize the signature of the Treasurer/CFO, or the facsimile thereof, to be used on all checks and warrants;

AND FURTHER RESOLVE to designate the Superintendent or her designee as purchasing agent for the Brecksville-Broadview Heights City Schools for 2020;

AND FURTHER RESOLVE to recognize the Superintendent or her designee as the appeal officer for out-of-school suspensions;

AND FURTHER RESOLVE to authorize the Superintendent to hire personnel and accept resignations on behalf of the board, pending final board approval;

ROLL CALL:  Mr. Dosen, yes; Mrs. Kramer, yes; Mrs. Mack, yes; Mr. Pedersen, yes; Mrs. Wigman, yes                   

The President declared Resolution 2020-04 adopted.
