Meeting Summary:

 

The Brecksville Broadview Heights Board of Education held a meeting on October 16, 2024, at the Brecksville Broadview Heights Education Center. The meeting included the Pledge of Allegiance, roll call, and various reports. Key highlights included the Cuyahoga Valley Career Center's new building for the construction trades program, which addresses workforce trends and student interest. The program has a significant waitlist and received a substantial grant to cover building costs. The Success Academy program was also discussed, which supports students identified as poor performers in attendance or academics, helping them become more successful. 

 

The meeting also covered updates from the Superintendent, and various committees, including Financial and Audit, Permanent Improvement, Policy, Transportation Appeals, Safety and Security, School's Foundation, Sustainability, and PSO reports. The Superintendent announced the State of the Schools event, community newsletter updates, and the upcoming Blue Ribbon ceremony in Washington, D.C.

 

The Board approved several items, including a service agreement for textile recycling, a wraparound expert service agreement for St. Columbkille, clinical experiences agreement with Ohio University, overnight trips for the high school robotics and athletic teams, and the district calendars for 2025-2027. Personnel recommendations were also approved, including the introduction of a new operations coordinator, Rachel Blanchette.

 

The Treasurer provided financial updates, including the approval of new funds and the acceptance of a bid for the Hilton Elementary School demolition project. The meeting concluded with the approval of donations and the minutes from the previous meeting.

The next regular meeting is scheduled for November 20, 2024.
