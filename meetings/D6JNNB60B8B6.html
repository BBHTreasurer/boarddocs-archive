Meeting Summary:

 

This is a summary from a meeting of the Brecksville Broadview Heights Education Center Board of Education on July 17th, 2024.

 

-The meeting was held to conduct the school district's business and was live streamed on YouTube. The meeting began with the Pledge of Allegiance and a roll call.

-The board discussed various topics including the voucher system, board appointments, superintendent committees reports, and the school's foundation report. The board unanimously agreed that the voucher system is broken but decided not to join the lawsuit. The board also discussed the upcoming   audit, the demolition of Hilton, and changes to the class rank policy.

-The superintendent's announcements included updates on class sizes and enrollment trends. The treasurer's announcements included a discussion on property taxes and the impact of property value increases on the district's revenue.

-The board approved several personnel recommendations, including the hiring of new teachers and the approval of supplemental personnel recommendations. The board also authorized the advertisement of bid specification for the demolition of Hilton Elementary.

-The meeting concluded with the announcement of the next meeting scheduled for August 21, 2024.
