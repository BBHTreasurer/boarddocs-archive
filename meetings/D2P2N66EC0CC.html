Following the Regular Meeting, the Board will meet in Executive Session for the purpose of preparing for, conducting, or reviewing negotiations or bargaining sessions with public employees concerning their compensations or other terms and conditions of their employment, item E per Board Policy 0166 - EXECUTIVE SESSION.
