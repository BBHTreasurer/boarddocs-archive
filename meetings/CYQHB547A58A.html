Special Meeting  (Wednesday, January 3, 2024)

 

 
1. Opening Items

 A. Pledge of Allegiance

 

Mr. Dosen called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m.

 

Present: Mr. Dosen, Mrs. Galek, Mrs. Kramer, Mrs. Kwiatkowski, Ms. O'Mara

 

Resolution 2024-16 - Executive Session

Adjournment

 

2. Executive Session

Motion by Mrs. Kwiatkowski, seconded by Mrs. Kramer to enter into Executive Session at 6:02 p.m. to adopt Resolution 2024-16 as follows: 

  A. Motion to enter into Executive Session per Ohio Revised Code 121.22, item G for the purpose of: preparing for, conducting, or reviewing negotiations or bargaining sessions with public employees concerning their compensations or other terms and conditions of their employment, per Board Policy 0166, item E.

  A. Adjournment

ROLL CALL: Mr. Dosen, yes; Mrs. Galek, yes; Mrs. Kramer, yes; Mrs. Kwiatkowski, yes; Ms. O'Mara, yes
