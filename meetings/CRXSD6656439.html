Minutes

Special Meeting  (Monday, May 22, 2023)

 

1. Opening Items

 A. Pledge of Allegiance

 

Mr. Dosen called the meeting to order at 5:30 p.m.

 

Present: Mrs. Kramer, Mrs. Kwiatkowski, Mrs. Mack, Mrs. Wigman, Mr. Dosen

 

Resolution 2023 - 46 - Resignation of Superintendent

Resolution 2023 - 47 - Appointment of Superintendent and Approval of Employment Contract

Announcement of Meetings

Adjournment

 

2. Recommendation of the Board of Education

Motion by Mrs. Kwiatkowski, seconded by Mrs. Wigman, to adopt Resolution 2023-46 as follows: 

 A. Resignation of Superintendent

ROLL CALL: Mrs. Kramer, yes; Mrs. Kwiatkowski, yes; Mrs. Mack, yes; Mrs. Wigman, yes; Mr. Dosen, yes

The President declared Resolution 2023-46 adopted. 

 

Motion by Mrs. Wigman, seconded by Mrs. Kramer, to adopt Resolution 2023-47 as follows:

 B. Appointment of Superintendent and Approval of Employment Contract

ROLL CALL: Mrs. Kramer, yes; Mrs. Kwiatkowski, yes; Mrs. Mack, yes; Mrs. Wigman, yes; Mr. Dosen, yes

The President declared Resolution 2023-47 adopted. 

 

3. Announcement of Meetings

 A. Regular Board Meeting ~ Wednesday, May 24, 2023 at 7:00 p.m.

 

4. Closing Items 

 A. Adjournment

Motion by Mrs. Wigman, seconded by Mrs. Kwiatkowski, to adjourn the Special Meeting at 5:36 p.m.

ROLL CALL: Mrs. Kramer, yes; Mrs. Kwiatkowski, yes; Mrs. Mack, yes; Mrs. Wigman, yes; Mr. Dosen, yes

                        

Motion carried.
