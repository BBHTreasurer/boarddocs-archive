Special Meeting 4:00 p.m.

May 11, 2021

Education Center4:00 PM~The BBHCSD Board of Education will meet via Zoom on Tuesday, May 11, 2021 for a Special Meeting at 4:00 PM. The Special Meeting will be held in public for the purpose of conducting the School District's Business and is not to be considered a public community meeting. This Special meeting will be held to discuss the Domestic Hot Water at the High School. There will be no public participation. The Special Meeting will be live streamed on the District's Youtube Channel and recorded for later viewing. https://youtu.be/dgJJgk_8Tso~Prior to adjourning, the Board will meet in Executive Session for the consideration of the appointment, employment, discipline, promotion, demotion or compensation of a public employee or official, per Board policy 0166, item A.
