July 6, 2021

Education Center5:00 p.m. The BBHCSD Board of Education will be holding a Special Meeting on Tuesday, July 6, 2021 at 5pm at 6638 Mill Road, Brecksville, OH 44141. This is a Special Meeting of the Brecksville-Broadview Heights Board of Education in public for the purpose of considering a resolution to approve the tentative agreement between the Brecksville-Broadview Heights City School District Board of Education and the Brecksville-Broadview Heights Education Association.The meeting will be livestreamed on the District's Youtube Channel: https://youtu.be/V0hhY45AewA
