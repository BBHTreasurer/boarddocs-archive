Executive Session Only 4:30 p.m. (Monday, March 1, 2021)

 

 A. Pledge of Allegiance

 

Mr. Pedersen called the meeting to order at 4:39 p.m.

 

Present: Mr. Dosen, Mrs. Kramer, Mrs. Wigman, Mr. Pedersen 

             Mrs. Mack, Absent

 

Resolution 2021-29 - Executive Session

Announcement of Meetings

Adjournment

 

2. Executive Session     

Motion by Mr. Dosen, seconded by Mrs. Kramer to enter Executive Session at 4:40 p.m.

to adopt Resolution 2021-29 as follows:

   

Motion to enter into Executive Session per Ohio Revised Code 121.22, item G for the purpose of:
(1) To consider the appointment, employment, dismissal, discipline, promotion, demotion, or
compensation of a public employee or official, or the investigation of charges or complaints against
a public employee, official, licensee, or regulated individual, unless the public employee, official,
licensee, or regulated individual requests a public hearing.

 

ROLL CALL:  Mr. Dosen, yes; Mrs. Kramer, yes; Mrs. Mack, Absent; Mrs. Wigman, yes; 

                   Mr. Pedersen, yes      

The President declared Resolution 2021-29 adopted.

Motion by Mr. Dosen, seconded by Mrs. Wigman to exit Executive Session at 5:15 p.m.

 

3. Announcement of Meetings    

 A. Regular Board Meeting ~ March 24, 2021

ROLL CALL:  Mr. Dosen, yes; Mrs. Kramer, yes; Mrs. Mack, Absent; Mrs. Wigman, yes; 

                   Mr. Pedersen, yes
