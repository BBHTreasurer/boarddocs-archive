Special Meeting (Wednesday, January 5, 2022)

 

1. Opening Items

  A. Pledge of Allegiance

 

Mr. Dosen called the meeting to order at 5:36 p.m.

 

Present: Mr. Dosen, Mrs. Kramer(absent), Mrs. Kwiatkowski, Mrs. Mack, Mrs. Wigman

                          

Resolution 2022-17 - BBHHS Varsity Softball Tournament in Myrtle Beach, SC

Resolution 2022-18 - Executive Session

Announcement of Meetings

Adjournment

 

2. Special Meeting 

Motion by Mrs. Wigman, seconded by Mrs. Kwiatkowski, to adopt Resolution 2022-17 as follows:

3. Executive Session

Motion by Mrs. Wigman, seconded by Mrs. Kwiatkowski, to enter Executive Session at 5:39 p.m. to adopt Resolution 2022-18 as follows: 

A. Enter into Executive Session per Ohio Revised Code 121.22, item G for the purpose of: the consideration of the appointment, employment, discipline, promotion, demotion or compensation of a public employee or official, per Board policy 0166, item A.

ROLL CALL: Mr. Dosen, yes; Mrs. Kramer, absent; Mrs. Kwiatkowski, yes; Mrs. Mack, yes; Mrs. Wigman, yes 

The President declared Resolution 2022-18 adopted. 

 

Motion by Mrs. Wigman, seconded by Mrs. Kwiatkowski to exit Executive Session at 7:08 p.m.

 

4. Announcement of Meetings

    Regular Board Meeting ~ January 19, 2022

 

8. Adjournment
