Special Meeting

August 10, 2021

Special MeetingTuesday, August 10, 2021High School Auditorium6:00 p.m.The BBHCSD Board of Education will be holding a Special Meeting on Tuesday, August 10, 2021 at 6pm at 6380 Mill Road, Brecksville, OH 44141. This is a Special Meeting of the Brecksville-Broadview Heights Board of Education in public for the purpose of having public participation in a discussion regarding COVID-19 practices for the 2021-2022 school year and to consider a resolution to allow for such participation at a Special Meeting; and for the purpose of considering a resolution to approve the recommended hire of certain retired teachers, and for the purpose of considering a resolution to approve other personnel recommendations. The meeting will be livestreamed on the District's Youtube Channel and directly linked: https://youtu.be/GnhA0f4WqpMBoard Agenda
